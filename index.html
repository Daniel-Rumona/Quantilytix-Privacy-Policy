1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, contact details.
  • Business Data: Sales, inventory, customer, financial data.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS, IP address.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, analytics, AI chatbot usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Operate and improve the app.
  • Provide AI-powered services.
  • Personalize user experience.
  • Conduct analytics and optimize performance.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your data is confidential and shared only:

  • With third-party service providers for functionality.
  • For legal compliance.
  • In case of business transfers.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, but no system is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain data as long as needed for services or legal compliance.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and update your data.
  • Delete your account.
  • Withdraw consent.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your account by following these steps:

  1. Go to the Profile page in the app.
  2. Select "View Details".
  3. Click "Delete Account and Data".

8. Minor’s Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 18.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Updates will be posted in the app and on our website.
